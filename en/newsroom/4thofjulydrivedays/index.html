Celebrating America's Birthday with $60 Drive Days

June 28, 2017

SHARE:

ReachNow on Facebook (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Linked-in (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Twitter (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Email (opens in a new window)
Fireworks, BBQs and road trips are just a few of our favorite things about celebrating the Fourth of July. With the weather heating up in the PNW, take advantage of special 4th of July Weekend pricing to explore the Northwest’s best camping, hiking and fireworks displays.

All weekend, drive for just $60 per 24 hours (plus applicable taxes and fees). That’s a 25% discount off our normal daily rate. This special rate is running from Friday through Tuesday, so get a jump start on the holiday weekend by grabbing your favorite BMW or MINI in your neighborhood.

Using ReachNow to head out of town? Here’s a couple pro tip’s as you pack-up for the weekend:
  • Don’t forget your Member Key Card if you’re headed out of the Home Area. There are plenty of off-the-beaten-path destinations to explore and having your Member Key Card can help avoid any service disruptions.
  • Planning to take a Ferry? Check out our guidance on how to take ReachNow cars on the Ferry (aka: bring your Member Key Card!).
  • Select “Park and Come Back:” If you’re planning to take a rental for a day or two, remember to select “Park and Come Back” to avoid multiple trip charges (and assure you get the best rate for the total rental).
More details on pricing below. From our ReachNow family to yours, we hope you have a wonderful and safe holiday weekend! 

 

HOW OUR 4TH OF JULY SPECIAL WORKS

To quality, start and end your trip between Friday (June 30) at 12:01am PT and end by Tuesday (July 4) at 11:59pm PT. Each trip will be capped at $60 for up to 24 hours. The reduced 24-hour flat rate is only applicable to continuous trips, so be sure to select "Park and Come Back" to qualify. This promotion is only valid for car sharing in Seattle and Portland. 


Terms & Conditions

Only valid for single continuous trip(s) – please select “Park and Come Back” until you are done with the reservation. Promotional pricing does not include applicable taxes and fees; only valid for ReachNow car sharing in Seattle and Portland. You are fully responsible for any remaining Rental Cost after the discount is redeemed and for any applicable taxes related to the Rental Cost. If you do not take a Qualifying Trip and successfully redeem this discount by the expiration date for this promotion listed above, the discount will expire and you will not be able to use it.
