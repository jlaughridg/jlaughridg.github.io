Hey Seattle, Don't Circle for Parking this Summer

June 15, 2017

SHARE:

ReachNow on Facebook (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Linked-in (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Twitter (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Email (opens in a new window)

It wouldn’t be summer in Seattle without an incredible line-up of outdoor festivals. From this weekend’s Fremont Fair to Capitol Hill Block Party, there’s so much to look forward to this summer. The only thing that can put a damper on all the fun? Circling for parking or massive surge pricing. 

Today, we’re happy to announce that we’re bringing Drop Zone to four of the summer’s most popular events, giving ReachNow members access to exclusive access to FREE parking just minutes from the action. 

You can find us at:

Drop Zone at Fremont Fair
June 17 – 18
743 N. 35th Street (map)
Next to the Fremont Library

TRAVEL NOTE: Coming from the north, the best way to get there will be to head south on Stone Way N and turn right at N 35th St. Coming from the south, take the Bridge Way N exit off of Highway 99, turn right on Stone Way N, and take another right on N 35th St.

Drop Zone at Capitol Hill PrideFest
June 24
Capitol Hill – Rancho Bravo Tacos
10th Ave and & E Pine St (map)
 
 
Drop Zone at Ballard Seafood Festival
July 7 – 9
22nd Ave NW (map)
Between Shilshole Ave NW and Ballard Ave NW
 
Drop Zone at Capitol Hill Block Party
July 21 – 23
1407 Harvard Ave (map)
Across from QFC
 
What’s a Drop Zone? In Seattle, we know that one of the most challenging things about getting out and about during the summer is limited parking. ReachNow Drop Zones are free, dedicated parking exclusively for ReachNow cars just steps from the action. Just a little something special for members.
 
We’ll send you a reminder before each event and we’re looking forward to seeing each of you this summer.
