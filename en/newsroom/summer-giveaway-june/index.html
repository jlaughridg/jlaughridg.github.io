Summer Giveaway: Win a Weekend with a BMW X1 and Other Great Prizes

June 7, 2017

SHARE:

ReachNow on Facebook (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Linked-in (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Twitter (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Email (opens in a new window)

Summer’s nearly here and we can hardly wait! To celebrate the start of the new season, we’re giving away some awesome prizes to five lucky members in each of our cities. Simply take a drive between today Wednesday June 7th and Wednesday June 14th for a chance to win, the more you drive, the better your chances of winning.*

First Prize, 3 Winners: A Long Weekend with a BMW X1 – we’ll give you a complimentary three day ReachNow rental. 1 winner per city.

Second Prize, 3 Winners: $100 in ReachNow credits, a ReachNow hoodie and even more swag. 1 winner per city.

Third Prize, 9 Winners: $50 in ReachNow credits. 3 winners per city.

Don’t forget to tell your friends about our summer giveaway! Have a great drive.

 

HOW IT WORKS, FULL TERMS AND CONDITIONS:

TO ENTER: (1) Take at least one ReachNow Car Sharing Trip during the eligibility period OR (2) Email promotions@reachnow.com with the subject line ‘SummerNow’ and include your name, address and phone number, during the eligibility period. Eligibility period begins Wednesday 6/7/17 12:01AM PDT and ends Wednesday 6/14/17 11:59PM PDT. No purchase is necessary to be entered. Must be a ReachNow Member to participate. Sign up to be a member for free by downloading the ReachNow App for iOS or Android and registering. Limit one entry per Member per day during eligibility period.

FIRST PRIZE: A complimentary 72 hour ReachNow rental of a BMW X1 (valued at $240 (SEA/PDX), or $250 (NYC)). Presented in form of promotion code valid through 12/31/2017. 3 total winners will be chosen consisting of 1 Winner in Seattle, WA, 1 Winner in Portland, OR, and 1 Winners in Brooklyn, NY.

SECOND PRIZE: $100 in ReachNow credits (credits expire 12/31/2017) and ReachNow branded sweatshirt and other swag. Hoodie and swag selection based upon availability and valued at $50. 3 total winners will be chosen consisting of 1 Winner in Seattle, WA, 1 Winner in Portland, OR, and 1 Winners in Brooklyn, NY.

THIRD PRIZE: $50 in ReachNow credits, credits expire 12/31/2017. 9 total winners will be chosen consisting of 3 Winners in Seattle, WA, 3 Winners in Portland, OR, and 3 Winners in Brooklyn, NY.

THE FINE PRINT: No purchase is necessary to be entered. Must be a ReachNow Member to participate. Sign up to be a member for free by downloading the ReachNow App for iOS or Android and registering. Total retail value of prize is stated above. Limit one entry per member per day permitted. Odds of winning are dependent on the number of entries received. The number of entries into the drawing is determined by the number of Members that (1) take a ReachNow Car Sharing Trip during the eligibility period (only ReachNow Car Sharing trips qualify, ReachNow  and (2) Number of Members that email promotions@reachnow.com during the eligibility period with the subject line “SummerNow” including full name, mailing address and phone number in email. Drawing will be held on Friday, June 16, 2017. Winner(s) will be chosen at random from all entries received. Winner(s) will be contacted by email, and prize will be mailed to the winner(s). If no response is received within 24 hours that winning entry is forfeited, a new winner will be randomly chosen. Participants may request a list of winners by emailing promotions@reachnow.com after June 19, 2017.
