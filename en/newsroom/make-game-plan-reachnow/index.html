Make A Game Plan
Drive All Day Sunday for $51

To mark the occasion of the 51st big game, we’re reducing our maximum daily rate to just $51 on Sunday, February 5th.

Whether you plan on taking advantage of the empty streets, enjoying the plethora of parking and no lines, stocking up the fridge for a get-together at home or scouting out the perfect place to watch the finale of football season, we’ll make sure you’re ahead of the game.

Please note: this special pricing will only be applied to single continuous trips. To take advantage of this offer on Sunday, select ‘Park and Come Back’ on the in-car display before you exit the vehicle. Only end your trip when you are done driving for the day.

***

Special pricing only valid for trips started on Sunday February 5th, 2017. Trips include 400 miles per day, additional mileage billed at $0.45 per mile. To redeem special rate cap, take a Qualifying Trip and pricing will be automatically applied. Rate is not inclusive of applicable taxes and fees. Rate cap only valid on a single continuous trip. You are fully responsible for any remaining Rental Cost after the discount is redeemed and for any applicable taxes related to the Rental Cost. Pricing subject to change. Capitalized terms not defined above have the definitions ascribed to them in the ReachNow Membership Agreement you signed as a condition of membership in the ReachNow car sharing program. You can obtain additional information regarding this promotion by calling (844) 732-2466 or by going to www.reachnow.com.
