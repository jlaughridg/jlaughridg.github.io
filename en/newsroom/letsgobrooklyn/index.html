Built for Brooklyn: New Weekday Pricing and Counterless Rentals are Here

May 23, 2017

SHARE:

ReachNow on Facebook (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Linked-in (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Twitter (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Email (opens in a new window)

Hello, Brooklyn! It’s been almost six months since our BMWs and MINIs first took the streets of Brooklyn. We’ve loved hearing your stories of weekend adventures Upstate and rekindling friendships otherwise lost to bus transfers. Your feedback is so valuable to us.

That’s what makes our news today extra special. We are excited to tell you about a few of the things that have been keeping us busy:

Now serving: Bay Ridge, Midwood, Ditmas Park & Flatbush

It’s time to kick things up a notch. We’re happy to announce that Bay Ridge, Midwood, Ditmas Park and Flatbush are now part of our Home Area. With this expansion, we’re now covering 20% more of Brooklyn with our Home Area.  

Our friends in Midwood, Ditmas Park, and Flatbush now have a convenient one-way option to avoid multiple bus transfers or long walks when you have your hands full. In fact, we’re the ONLY car sharing service now serving The Brooklyn College community.

New Weekday Pricing: $25 for up to 3 hours* 

We also heard from many of you about the ways that you wish you could use ReachNow more. At the top of that list? Mid-week activities.

While we know better than to mess with your morning commute routine, you did hint that spicing up your mid-day and post-work routine may be just what the doctor ordered. Starting today, we’re offering special weekday pricing only for our Brooklyn members. Rent any ReachNow car for up to 3 hours for only $25 (a 50% discount). At that price, you can hit a real grocery store rather than a bodega and still make it to the other end of the borough in time to meet up with friends—all for less than the cost of an Uber to Midtown.  The fine print? All trips starting between Monday at 9:00am ET and Thursday at 6:00pm ET will qualify for this reduced rate.  Not so bad, ya?

(No) Weekend plans? Avoid the counter with ReachNow’s Counterless Rentals

On a summer Friday, the only thing on your mind is the fastest way to get from work and to [insert your dream destination]. We know you’re spending too much money – and worse, time – getting to and/from car rental locations, on the Jitney or the train.

All summer long, rent your favorite ReachNow on demand for as low as $70 per day. Day rates start at $110/day and get lower the more days you keep it. No counters. No lines. No upsell. More details here.

At those prices, ReachNow is ideal for the trip planner or a spontaneous day-trip to Coney Island or Sandy Hook. Keep an eye on your inbox tomorrow for more detail on a special deal we have planned for Memorial Day.

Phew, ok. That was a lot to cover, but we hope you’ll agree – things are starting to really heat up here. So, what are you waiting for? Let’s go.
