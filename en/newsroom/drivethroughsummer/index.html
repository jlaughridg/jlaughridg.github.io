Drive Through Summer Rewards Program

July 1, 2017

SHARE:

ReachNow on Facebook (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Linked-in (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Twitter (opens in a new window) ReachNow on Email (opens in a new window)

We’re excited to introduce you to our invite-only Drive Through Summer rewards program. We’re rewarding our most loyal members with free driving credit to use on trips in August and September. How can you participate? Simply take a drive to be automatically entered, and keep driving – 3 or more times before August 13th. You will earn $10 in driving credit for every 3 drives you take by 8/13, up to $50 in driving credits!

HOW IT WORKS

From July 13th through August 13th, participating Drive Through Summer members will earn $10 in ReachNow credits for every 3 drives they take, up to $50. Promotional credits will be delivered via email on August 16th and valid for use through September 22, 2017.

Questions? Reach out to promotions@reachnow.com.  

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

DRIVE THROUGH SUMMER TERMS & CONDITIONS

QUALIFYING MEMBER
Promotion only valid for approved ReachNow members who were invited via email by ReachNow. Qualifying Members will be automatically entered to participate in the program by taking Qualifying Trips during the Promotional Period.

QUALIFYING TRIPS
The following trips qualify towards Drive Through Summer: Billed ReachNow car sharing drives lasting longer than 5 minutes, in Seattle, Portland and Brooklyn count towards Drive Through Summer Awards. Trips must occur during the Promotional Period. Trips using ReachNow Ride do not count towards Qualifying Award Tier. Qualifying Trips cannot be combined, accrued or transferred from multiple accounts (including those of family members).

PROMOTIONAL PERIOD
Promotional Period open July 13, 2017 through August 13, 2017, trip(s) must end by 11:59pm PST on August 13, 2017 to qualify.

AWARD CREDITS
To receive Award credits, Qualifying Members must take a minimum of 3 trips during the Promotional Period, up to a maximum of 15 trips. Award credits will only be issued at the Qualifying Award Tiers listed below. There will be no partial Award if member makes trips more than or less than the Qualifying Award Tiers. For example, if a member makes 2 Qualifying Trips during the Promotional Period, there will be no Award credit issued. If a member makes 18 trips during the Promotional Period, there will be no additional credits (above $50) issued.

Award credits will be delivered via email on Wednesday August 16, 2017 to the email address on file of the Qualifying Member. Award Credits will be valid for use until September 22, 2017, at which point they will expire and Qualifying Member shall forfeit remaining value.
