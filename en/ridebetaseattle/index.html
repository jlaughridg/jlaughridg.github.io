ReachNow Ride FAQs

What is ReachNow Ride? 

ReachNow Ride is ride sharing from ReachNow for the times you don’t feel like driving yourself. The ReachNow Ride Beta is now available to all Seattle ReachNow members. With Ride, members can request or schedule a ride in a BMW from ReachNow. Ride has no surge pricing. We've built in additional personalization features that let you create the perfect Ride before you get in - preset the temperature, radio station and even try our “Do Not Disturb” mode for when you need a quiet moment.

How do I join the ReachNow Ride beta?

Join ReachNow. Get started by downloading the ReachNow app for iOS or Android.
Already a member? Download the ReachNow+ Beta App with the links above.
Just joined? Check your inbox in about a week. All Seattle members will be invited with a week. 
 

How much does ReachNow Ride cost?

ReachNow Ride pricing is based on three elements: a per trip base fare of $1.00, a per minute rate of $0.30 and per mile rate of $1.80. As with any shared service, applicable taxes and fees are applied. You will receive a fare estimate before you request any ride and we do not have surge pricing.
 

Interested in driving for ReachNow Ride?

Get connected: ride@reachnow.com. 

What you should expect

You're using ReachNow Ride in beta mode. By using it, you're helping us build the service that will eventually roll out in other ReachNow cities. And, because you're helping us to test, you'll experience a few bugs and a few features that are in test mode. 

How to help

Just shake the ReachNow+ Beta App to tell us what you love (or maybe don't love as much). You can also leave feedback when you rate your ride. 
 
The more feedback the better, so don't be shy.
